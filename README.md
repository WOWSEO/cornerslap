# CornerSlap

**Own your corner of the world.**

Free website builder, POS system, and link in bio platform for small businesses. Works in 138 countries and 49 languages.

[cornerslap.com](https://cornerslap.com) | [@cornerslapai](https://instagram.com/cornerslapai)

## What is CornerSlap?

CornerSlap gives any small business a professional website, a point of sale system that runs on your phone, online ordering, and an AI assistant for $0/month.

- **Website builder**: AI builds your site in 60 seconds. No coding.
- **POS system**: Runs on any phone. Accept Apple Pay, Google Pay, cards, and cash. No hardware needed.
- **Link in bio**: Smart overlay bar with Order Online, Find Us (GPS map), Events, Live Music, Share, and 49-language selector.
- **Online ordering**: Customers order from your site. Orders appear in your POS.
- **Delivery integrations**: DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub, Deliveroo, iFood, Rappi, and 39 more.
- **Kitchen display**: Orders stream to a tablet in real time.
- **AI assistant**: Voice-powered employee that handles menu updates, social posts, sales reports, expense tracking, and daily operations.
- **138 countries, 49 languages**: Local currency, local delivery apps, RTL support.

## Pricing

The business pays $0. No monthly fee. No setup fee. No hardware fee. No contracts.

Customers pay a 1 cent per dollar service fee on card payments. Cash is completely free. The business keeps 100% of every sale.
